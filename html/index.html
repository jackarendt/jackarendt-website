iOS DEVELOPMENT

"Keep it simple, stupid." These are the words that I live by. Since I started developing for iOS in June of 2013, I have always designed my applications with simplicity in mind. Apps shouldnt be bloated with useless or confusing features that clutter the interface. I'm always trying to push the limits of usability and the user experience.

PROJECTS

ODDS ARE

Odds Are started off as a series of mockups for a UI/UX design course, but developed in to a full featured application. The app combines the excitement of daring your friends with social, and allows you to keep up with what your friends are daring each other to do on the newsfeed. The app is written in Swift 1.2 and has been live on the App Store since July 2015. I wrote the entire iOS application, and created all mockups prior to release. Our server is currently Parse, which uses Backbone.js for server logic, and sending out APNs.

PIPELINE

Pipeline is a pipe-based maze game. Combine all the pipe pieces to connect the pipe. The only caveat is that all the pieces need to be used to complete the level! There are currently 96 levels on Pipeline. It was released on the App Store in February 2015, and has been downloaded in over fifteen countries. The app is written in Objective-C and uses Core Data to store all level/user data.
